Fyodor Weiss

While I was only into Twisted Wonderland for a few months back in 2020, my TWST OC had a pretty big impact on my creative output during that time and even influenced the design of my next OC in FFXIV! Named Fyodor Weiss, he is a white tiger animalfolk who transferred from Savanaclaw dorm to Octavinelle.

The idea for this character came from my own experience playing the game, at first drawn to Savanaclaw due to The Lion King being one of my favourite things ever, but quickly finding that the characters and aesthetics of Octavinelle were much more appealing to me, even though at the time I had not seen the film it was inspired by. There was also a bit of "fanon" about the three main Octavinelle characters that went into shaping my vision for Fyodor!

His name originates from "Theodore", rendered in its Russian form "Fyodor"; I thought that, since his animal inspiration is a Siberian tiger, a Russian name would fit well. Initially I wasn't a big fan of this version of the name, but it definitely grew on me! His surname, "Weiss", is simply "white" in German, because he is a white tiger; my idea was that it wouldn't look too out of place in such a game to have names of different origins mixed like that, given how wacky a lot of the characters' names are haha.

Having been created right in the middle of the pandemic during the height of my creative career, a lot of Fyodor's characteristics were based on myself. I am no longer following TWST and updating Fyodor, but below the gallery I've included both his formal biography and all my rambling tweets I could find about him!

He disliked being forced to do things at school, even if he was good at them; for instance, being of the tiger kin he was naturally strong, but hated P.E. classes because of the sports and teamwork activities. He preferred to learn things on his own and at his own pace, and as such he developed his unique magic as a way of escaping situations he found uncomfortable. Fyodor was also invested in hero shows and films, though he often looked up to the villains instead, whom he found much cooler and more interesting. Much of his introvertedness stemmed from his fear of being rejected because of his interests and nerdiness, which led to a constant feeling of being out of place.

Although he avoided a good portion of the school activities, he wasn‚Äôt averse to authority and had a generally polite disposition. His magic skills were decent but rarely showcased, as he disliked being in the spotlight. Nevertheless, he was chosen to study at Night Raven College and was quickly sorted into Savanaclaw, as most animalfolk are.
