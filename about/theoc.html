About Neo, the OC!

In the long-forgotten year of 2013, I was in the first year of high school and had just started taking a liking to Chemistry. It was in August that I discovered a quirky little book written and illustrated by a Japanese artist named B.Y., which presented the elements of the periodic table personified, with different bodies, hairstyles and personalities that reflected their properties. People that are into Chemistry tend to have a favourite element, and thanks to said book I found the one for me. Dubbed "the world's strongest magnet", neodymium became my new favourite element, a decision that would forever change my life!

I was very drawn to its design in the book, which I won't reproduce here for copyright reasons. "He" looked so cool with his pointy hair, confident expression and yellow-and-black body representing a magnet, not to mention the wide variety of essential uses the 60th element has in current technology.

A year later, in August of 2014, the ne0dym was born. I first coined the username on tumblr, and it remains my online handle to this day.

Having adopted him as my "persona" and my son, I got my first commission done by the brilliant siins, who would be a major influence on his design going forward. This first commi was simply based on the original illustration in the book, yet throughout 2015 I continued to tweak his appearance merging elements of siins's artwork with other traits I came to enjoy.

By early 2016, it was time to request an updated portrait, and I consider this the true genesis of Neo, the OC! The two commissions by siins from this period had a few defining features that would become permanent: a hoodie with inverted colours to the sweater, big chompers, pointy ears, and facial hair that would only grow with time. At the end of that year, I commissioned Skelizard to draw Neo's first full-body artwork.

In 2017, a new piece by siins became his canon design. This new Neo looked much like the previous one, except he grew a beard and became even more monster-like. I also commissioned several other artists to draw their take on my character, building a small collection of art pieces of my beloved persona. In 2018, I even got my very own unique Neo plushie, created by an acquaintance, Krill! Many people have lent their talents to Neo in commissions and gifted pieces over the years, and I am very grateful to each one.

In 2019, I received my most recent siins commission, featuring a full beard and his now-iconic bottom tusks. In 2020, my friend Sidney created what I consider Neo's second canon illustration, so perfectly did he portray my character! And in 2023, Rossy delivered a charming piece of Neo in his most monsteriness.
